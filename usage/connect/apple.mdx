---
title: "Connect an Apple device"
description: "Connect macOS, iOS, and tvOS devices to your headscale instance using the official Tailscale clients."
---

<Info>
  Step-by-step instructions for connecting your Apple device are also available directly on your running headscale instance at `/apple`.
</Info>

## iOS

### Install the client

Install the official [Tailscale iOS client](https://apps.apple.com/app/tailscale/id1470499037) from the App Store.

### Configure the headscale URL

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Tailscale app">
    Launch Tailscale on your iOS device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start login">
    Tap the account icon in the top-right corner and select **Log in…**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a custom coordination server">
    Tap the top-right options menu button and select **Use custom coordination server**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your instance URL">
    Enter your headscale instance URL (e.g., `https://headscale.example.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete login">
    Enter your credentials and log in. Headscale should now be working on your iOS device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## macOS

### Install the client

Choose one of the available [Tailscale clients for macOS](https://tailscale.com/kb/1065/macos-variants) and install it.

### Configure the headscale URL

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Command line">
    Use the Tailscale login command to connect to your headscale instance:

    ```shell
    tailscale login --login-server <YOUR_HEADSCALE_URL>
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GUI">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the debug menu">
        Option + Click the Tailscale icon in the menu bar and hover over the **Debug** menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add account">
        Under **Custom Login Server**, select **Add Account…**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter your instance URL">
        Enter the URL of your headscale instance (e.g., `https://headscale.example.com`) and press **Add Account**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete login">
        Follow the login procedure in the browser.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## tvOS

### Install the client

Install the official [Tailscale tvOS client](https://apps.apple.com/app/tailscale/id1470499037) from the App Store.

<Warning>
  Do **not** open the Tailscale app after installation. Configure the server URL first using the steps below.
</Warning>

### Configure the headscale URL

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Tailscale settings">
    Open **Settings** (the Apple tvOS settings) > **Apps** > **Tailscale**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the coordination server URL">
    Under **ALTERNATE COORDINATION SERVER URL**, select **URL**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your instance URL">
    Enter the URL of your headscale instance (e.g., `https://headscale.example.com`) and press **OK**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to the home screen">
    Return to the tvOS home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Tailscale">
    Open Tailscale, tap **Install VPN configuration**, and confirm the popup by tapping **Allow**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete login">
    Scan the QR code and follow the login procedure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
